What our Loyalty Scheme is:

Our loyalty scheme comes with a range of benefits, the first of these benefits is a discount on every CD buy, this discount is £1.00 per CD, which is a huge saving in the long run.
The second benefit is that you can recieve an email each month which contains a newsletter of the new releases we have in store, whether they are local releases or not.
The final benefit is a one-time-only offer: When you first apply to the loyalty scheme, you can recieve any single CD from the shop.
The overall price of the loyalty scheme is £20 per year, and there will be no further charges, unless you wish to renew the next year.
