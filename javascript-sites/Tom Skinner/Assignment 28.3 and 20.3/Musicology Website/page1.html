What is Musicology?

At Musicology, we are a Sheffield based company which sells music ranging between music from the charts to exclusive locally produced and released music. Our specialty is local music, sold at a reasonable price, it would be hard to match these prices. To see our products, click here.

Along with selling music, we also have a loyalty scheme, with this you can recieve all the new music news about new releases within Sheffield.

To access information to out location and how to contact us via Email or telphone, click here.

A goal of ours:

Our biggest goal is to assist in making local musicians become widely known by more than just people living here in Sheffield.

We do this by selling almost exlusively local releases on both vinyl and CD in the hope that the musician will become noticed by their music being available to whoever wishes to purchase it.

We sell a huge range of vinyl and CDs to fit all music tastes.

We work hard to promote local musicians as much as we can.
